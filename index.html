WHY ADOPT SENIOR DOGS?

Most people look for young dogs or puppies when wanting to adopt. Unfortunately, senior shelter pets across the U.S. are the least likely to ever find new homes.
Here are some great resons to adopt:

Reasons to adopt an older dog...

  1. Senior dogs are already housebroken.
  2. More mellow and require less exercise.
  3. Already trained and can still learn more!

When you’re thinking about picking up a dog from your local shelter, don’t look past the older dogs. They make great pets for a number of reasons!
Senior dogs are already housebroken. That's right, no need to have to potty-train the older ones! They already learned and it will save you the headache and the cleanups! Senior dogs are more mellow and require less exercise. You won't have to worry about coming home to find your sofa chewed up or a restless pup that requires an hour of exercise! Senior dogs are already trained and can still learn more. Dogs can be trained at any age and older dogs are just as smart as younger ones. Older dogs have a greater attention span than a puppy, which make them easier to train.
